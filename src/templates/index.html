PDF-to-MusicXML converter with Audiveris

This app converts a music score PDF into a .mxl-file with Audiveris 5.10.2 (built from upstream sources inside a Docker container). The conversion process may take several minutes (and it is recommended to use only a very small file). So keep calm & be patient...
